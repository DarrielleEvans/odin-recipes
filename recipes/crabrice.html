Crab and Rice

Description

If you love rice and want to add some flavor,>

the Crab and Rice dish is for you.

Crab and rice is flavored with onion and peppers in addition to a nice tast of crab meat.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup uncooked long grain white rice
  • 1 ⅓ cups water
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 medium onions, cut into wedges
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • ½ tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • <1 egg, beaten /li>
  • ¼ pound cooked crabmeat
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • <1 tablespoon chopped cilantro/li>
  • ½ cucumber, sliced
  • 1 lime, sliced

Steps

  1. Place the rice and water in a saucepan, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil in a wok over medium heat. Cook and stir the onions and garlic in the hot oil until tender.
    Mix in the rice, sugar, and salt, and cook 5 minutes, until well blended. Stir in the egg until the rice is coated.
    Increase heat to high, and mix in the crabmeat, green onions, and cilantro. Continue cooking 2 to 5 minutes,
    until the crab is heated through. Garnish with the cucumber and lime slices to serve.
